High-Performance Compression Springs for Industrial Applications

Compression springs are one of the most widely used mechanical components in industrial engineering. These open-coil helical springs function by compressing under applied force and returning to their original shape once the load is released. This property allows them to store energy, absorb shock, reduce vibrations, and maintain force between two surfaces. Their versatility makes them essential in a wide range of applications, including valves, actuators, shock absorbers, electrical switches, pumps, and heavy-duty machines. Compression springs are engineered for reliability, consistency, and durability in environments where repeated motion and varying stress levels are common.
