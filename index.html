Reliable Spring Manufacturer & Wholesale Supplier from Delhi NCR

HeliSpring Works is a trusted name in the Indian spring manufacturing industry, delivering precision-engineered spring solutions to clients across Delhi NCR and beyond. As a reliable spring manufacturer and wholesale supplier, we specialize in producing high-quality compression springs, tension springs, and custom spring assemblies for a wide variety of applications. Our extensive customer base spans key industrial areas like Faridabad, Ghaziabad, Noida, Gurugram, and Delhi, along with clients located throughout India.

With decades of manufacturing expertise and advanced production technologies, we ensure that each spring meets strict quality and dimensional standards. Our team designs and manufactures springs using premium materials like stainless steel (302, 304, 316), phosphor bronze, carbon steel, music wire, and brass to ensure long-lasting performance, corrosion resistance, and durability. Whether it’s a small batch for a specialized application or a bulk order for OEM production, we have the capacity and experience to meet your needs.

As a dedicated wholesale spring supplier, we support high-volume production runs for industries such as electronics, automotive, medical devices, electrical equipment, and consumer goods. Our facility is optimized for just-in-time manufacturing and large-scale distribution, ensuring that clients receive their orders on schedule without compromising on quality. From small compression springs used in battery holders and relays to tension springs in mechanical locks and industrial tools, we deliver reliable, tailor-made spring solutions for every use case.

Our commitment to excellence, responsive customer service, and deep industry knowledge make us the preferred spring manufacturer for businesses throughout Faridabad, Ghaziabad, Delhi NCR, and across India. If you’re looking for a trustworthy partner for your custom or bulk spring requirements, HeliSpring Works is ready to deliver with precision, consistency, and speed.
