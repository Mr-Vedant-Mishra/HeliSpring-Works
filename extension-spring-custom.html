Precision-Made Custom Extension Springs for Diverse Applications

Custom extension springs are vital components used across various industries to provide tension and mechanical force when stretched. These springs are engineered to meet exact specifications and are critical in applications where controlled force and durability are required. Industries such as automotive, aerospace, medical, construction, and home appliances rely on high-quality extension springs for reliable performance in demanding conditions. These springs are designed to absorb energy, store mechanical energy, or maintain tension within a system, making them indispensable for a wide range of mechanical systems.

At HeliSpring Works, we specialize in the production of custom extension springs that are built to deliver superior strength and flexibility. Using state-of-the-art manufacturing techniques such as CNC spring coiling and automated winding, we ensure each spring is crafted to precise tolerances. Our extension springs are available in various materials, including stainless steel, spring steel, carbon steel, and alloy steel. Whether you need corrosion-resistant springs for outdoor use or high-tensile springs for heavy-duty industrial applications, we provide customized solutions tailored to meet your unique requirements.

We understand that every project is unique, and that's why we offer additional services like design consultation and prototype development to help refine your product before mass production. Our experienced engineers work closely with clients to design springs that not only meet functional needs but also optimize cost-efficiency and production timelines. With a focus on precision and performance, we are committed to delivering extension springs that exceed expectations.
