Precision Conical Springs Manufacturer & Wholesale Supplier in Delhi NCR

Conical springs, also known as tapered compression springs or cone springs, are specialized helical springs designed with a variable coil diameter that decreases toward one end. This unique shape allows conical springs to compress in a telescoping fashion, reducing solid height and increasing stability under load. They are ideal for applications requiring minimal space and a consistent compression rate, such as in automotive components, electrical contacts, battery assemblies, and heavy industrial devices.

HeliSpring Works is a leading conical spring manufacturer and wholesale supplier based in Delhi NCR, providing reliable spring solutions to industries across Faridabad, Gurugram, Noida, and Ghaziabad. We produce a wide range of conical compression springs, including stainless steel conical springs (302, 304, 316), mild steel cone springs, small conical springs, hourglass springs, barrel-shaped springs, and double conical springs. Each spring is crafted with precision engineering to meet custom specifications for wire thickness, load capacity, coil count, and free length.

Our advanced CNC spring coiling machines and quality inspection systems ensure durability, corrosion resistance, and high fatigue life.
