Premium Extension Springs for Diverse Industrial Uses

Extension springs are vital mechanical components designed to provide resistance to tensile forces. These tightly wound helical springs are used to store and release energy when extended, offering flexibility in various industrial applications. When a load is applied, the spring stretches, and once the force is removed, the spring returns to its original length. This ability to resist stretching and return to shape makes extension springs ideal for shock absorption, tension control, and force regulation in systems that require precise movement.
